Idea

We are pleased to invite you to the first edition of the Nokia Volleyball Cup!

  • Date → 10 January 2015

  • Place → ul. Chełmońskiego 43, Wroclaw

  • Start time → 9:00 AM

Sign up your team and have fun during the tournament :) The only requirement is that you are a Nokia employee, but feel free to take your friends and family to support you in the stands!
